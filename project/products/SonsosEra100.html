Description

The Sonos Era 100 is a powerful and compact wireless smart speaker, delivering immersive stereo sound and deep bass from a single enclosure. It features Bluetooth connectivity, voice control, and seamless integration into the Sonos ecosystem for multi-room audio.

Specifications

  • Audio: Custom-designed driver, stereo sound with two tweeters
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.0, Apple AirPlay 2
  • Voice Control: Built-in support for Amazon Alexa and Sonos Voice Control
  • Microphones: Array for voice control and Trueplay tuning
  • Design: Compact, available in Black or White
  • Features: Stereo pairing, line-in capability (with adapter), Auto Trueplay tuning
