Description

The Huawei Watch GT 5 Pro is a premium smartwatch that combines elegant design with advanced health and fitness tracking features. It boasts an impressive battery life, a durable build, and a comprehensive set of sensors for monitoring various activities and wellness metrics.

Specifications

  • Display: 1.43-inch AMOLED, 466x466 resolution
  • Battery Life: Up to 14 days (typical use)
  • Health Monitoring: Heart rate, SpO2, sleep tracking, temperature sensor
  • Sports Modes: Over 100 workout modes
  • Durability: 5 ATM water resistance
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.2, GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, QZSS
  • Compatibility: Android and iOS (with limited features on iOS)
