Description

The Denon AVR-X1800H is a versatile 7.2-channel 8K AV receiver designed to deliver immersive home theater and high-fidelity audio experiences. It supports the latest video technologies and features advanced room calibration for optimal sound performance in any environment.

Specifications

  • Channels: 7.2-channel surround sound
  • Power Output: 80W per channel (8 ohms, 20Hz-20kHz, 0.08% THD)
  • Video Support: 8K/60Hz, 4K/120Hz pass-through, HDR10+, Dolby Vision, HLG
  • Audio Formats: Dolby Atmos, DTS:X, DTS Virtual:X, Auro-3D
  • Connectivity: 6 HDMI inputs (1 front), HEOS built-in for multi-room audio, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, Ethernet
  • Room Calibration: Advanced Audyssey MultEQ XT
  • Features: Voice control (Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, Apple Siri), Apple AirPlay 2
