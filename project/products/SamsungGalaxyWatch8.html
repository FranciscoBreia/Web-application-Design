Description

The Samsung Galaxy Watch 8 is a premium smartwatch powered by Wear OS, designed for comprehensive health tracking, fitness coaching, and seamless connectivity with the Samsung ecosystem. It features a sleek, durable design with a vibrant display and powerful new chipset for enhanced performance.

Specifications

  • Display: 1.3" or 1.5" Super AMOLED, Sapphire Crystal glass
  • Health Sensors: BioActive Sensor (ECG, BIA, Heart Rate), Skin Temperature Sensor, Blood Pressure Monitor*
  • Battery Life: Up to 40 hours (typical use)
  • Charging: Wireless charging
  • Durability: 5ATM + IP68, MIL-STD-810H compliant
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.3, Wi-Fi, LTE (optional)
  • OS: Wear OS 5 with One UI Watch
  • Processor: New Exynos W1000 chipset (3nm)

*Features like Blood Pressure Monitor and ECG may require a Samsung Galaxy smartphone and market approval.
