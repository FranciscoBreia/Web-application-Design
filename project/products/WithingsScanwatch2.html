Description

The Withings ScanWatch 2 is a premium hybrid smartwatch that combines a classic analog design with advanced health monitoring technology. It features medical-grade sensors, including ECG and SpO2 tracking, and boasts an exceptional 30-day battery life. Crafted with a stainless steel case and scratch-resistant sapphire glass.

Specifications

  • Display: Analog hands with OLED sub-display
  • Health Sensors: ECG, SpO2, Temperature, Heart Rate
  • Battery Life: Up to 30 days
  • Water Resistance: 5 ATM (50 meters / 165 feet)
  • Compatibility: iOS and Android (Withings Health Mate app)
  • GPS: Connected GPS (uses your phone's GPS)
