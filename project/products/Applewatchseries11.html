Description

The Apple Watch Series 11 is the latest iteration of Apple's premium smartwatch, featuring a sleek design, advanced health monitoring capabilities, and seamless integration with the Apple ecosystem. It offers enhanced performance, new health sensors, and innovative features for everyday use and fitness tracking.

Specifications

  • Display: 1.9-inch Always-On Retina LTPO OLED (expected)
  • Chip: New S10 SiP (System in Package) for faster performance
  • Health Sensors: ECG, Blood Oxygen (SpO2), Heart Rate, Temperature Sensing, Sleep Tracking
  • Battery Life: Up to 24 hours (typical use); possible extended battery mode
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.3, Wi-Fi 6, GPS/GNSS, Cellular option (LTE/5G)
  • Durability: 50m water resistance, IP6X dust resistance
  • Software: watchOS 11 with new fitness and health features
